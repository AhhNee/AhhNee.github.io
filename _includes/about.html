Originally from the far north of Germany, I came to Berlin to study Interface Design at the FH Potsdam, and began working at Service Design agency Fjord in 2011. As a Designer I am responsible for creating innovative services, products and strategies for clients from varying industries from all around the globe. I think of my job much like solving a puzzle, in that I must find simple solutions for complex problems, guide teams through every stage of the design process, balance user and business needs, and make sure that all these pieces come together in services with an outstanding user experience.

Apart from my design work, I spent as much time as possible on the outside with my two children, exploring nature and playgrounds in and around Berlin. I am fascinated by graphic novels, and use their style of visual storytelling as an inspiration on my designs. I keep a clear head through meditation, and by riding my bike on a daily basis.
